# Professional Market Analysis Review

You are coordinating a team of specialized trading analysts.

The task is to evaluate the section:

"Анализ ситуации на рынке"

Use the following subagents:

- macro-agent
- technical-agent
- risk-agent
- quant-agent
- logic-agent
- sentiment-agent
- strategy-agent

Each agent analyzes the text independently.

## Instructions

1. Send the analysis to each subagent.
2. Collect their reports.
3. Produce a consolidated professional evaluation.

## Final Output

### Overall Quality Score
Rate the analysis from 1–10.

### Macro Environment
Summary of macro-agent findings.

### Market Structure
Summary of technical-agent findings.

### Risk Evaluation
Summary of risk-agent findings.

### Quantitative Analysis
Summary of quant-agent findings.

### Logical Consistency
Summary of logic-agent findings.

### Market Sentiment
Summary of sentiment-agent findings.

### Strategy Evaluation
Summary of strategy-agent findings.

### Final Professional Verdict

Would this analysis be sufficient for real trading decisions?

### Recommended Improvements
Explain how the analysis should be improved.
